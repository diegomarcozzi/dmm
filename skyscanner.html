Skyscanner

Overview

I received an invitation from Skyscanner (EMEA) to design a responsive ad campaign to be displayed through the Google Display Network. This work was entirely done through Upwork (contract #21632167, Feb 2019).

My Role

Freelance Art Director.

Hats worn

  • UI Design

  • Art Direction

The Challenge

The main challenge was to design a responsive template that could be adapted to the +20 different types of banners that the Google Display Network has. This may sound simple but it is not, especially with the smaller sizes. There's also the readability issue, the brand guidelines concerning the position of the logo and its background, and an extra challenge: they wanted to be able to edit the prices.

I think it's also relevant to mention that the destinations were all different, so what worked for some might not look good on others.

For this job I used Illustrator, Photoshop and Google Web Designer.

Problem solving example: Exploration

Each project has a certain number of problems to solve. Some problems are visible right from the beginning and others arise along the way. The idea is to provide a practical example of my thinking process, problem-solving skills and execution.

Considering that the brand guidelines were exceptionally strict, I explored a certain number of concepts with the elements that were allowed. Although the example only shows one city, the same template had to work for all of them.

Sometimes you have to work a lot to achieve a concept that convinces everyone. In this case I was very lucky, because the client really liked one of my first designs, which after some minor modifications was the one chosen for the Skyscanner Summer 2019 Campaign.

I assembled the delivered files in such a way that the price could be easily edited from a single line of html. I could have delivered it as it was, however I went a step further by documenting and adding notes to each file and code segment as the idea was to make it so simple that even a person with no coding experience could be able to edit it.
